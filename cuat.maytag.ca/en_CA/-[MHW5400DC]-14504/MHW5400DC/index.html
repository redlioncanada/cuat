Additional Features...

Designed, Engineered and Assembled in Clyde, Ohio

From Clyde, Ohio to you, Maxima® washers are designed, engineered and assembled in the U.S.A.

10-Year Limited Parts Warranty2 on the Direct Drive Motor and Stainless Steel Wash Basket

All Maxima® washers are covered by a 10-year limited parts warranty2 on the direct drive motor and stainless steel wash basket, so you know when we say you can count on Maxima® washers for years of cleaning performance, we mean it.

Maytag® Commercial Technology

Maxima XL™ & Maxima X™ washers bring long-lasting, commercial-grade components that you can depend on into your home. The pump impeller, corrosion-resistant springs and dampers are all built to easily handle load after load, week after week.

Fresh Hold® Option

The Fresh Hold® option lets you wash on your schedule. It helps keep clothes fresh for up to 12 hours after the cycle has ended.

Steam-Enhanced Cycles

Enhanced cycles use the power of steam so that your washer can really stick it to stains.

Steam for Stains option

Available with select wash cycles including Allergen and PowerWash™, this option uses a combination of water temperature and steam to provide the optimal environment for detergent enzymes to work on removing organic stains.

Sanitize Cycle

The Sanitize cycle on select Maxima® washers removes 99.9% of household bacteria4.

4.8 Cu. Ft. I.E.C. Capacity

The 4.8 cu. ft. I.E.C. capacity of Maxima® washers is designed to tackle your toughest loads.

Direct Drive Motor

Our washers feature a direct drive motor to get the job done no matter what's in the wash basket.

Stainless Steel Wash Basket

Resists scratches and rust to help prevent the snagging and staining of clothes.

Cold Wash Cycle

The Cold Wash cycle gives you the energy savings and gentle cleaning power of cold water.

Allergen cycle

Do more than just clean clothes with this cycle, which eliminates at least 95% of common allergens from fabrics.

Advanced Vibration Control™ (FL)

Wash loads of nearly any size without worrying about vibration. A combination of a ball-bearing balance ring, a six-point suspension system and advanced software easily handle the vibration of almost any load size.

Wrinkle Control Cycle

Maxima® washers have a Wrinkle Control cycle that is designed to help keep wrinkles from forming in the wash.

Cycle Memory

Once you've found your favorite stain-fighting options on each cycle, Maxima® washers automatically apply them the next time you use that cycle.

Rapid Wash Cycle

The Rapid Wash cycle washes small loads quickly for those times when your must-have outfit looks less than presentable.
