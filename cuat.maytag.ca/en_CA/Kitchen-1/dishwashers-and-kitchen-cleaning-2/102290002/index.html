    Dishwashers

  • Traditional Console
    Dishwashers

    • The most powerful motor on the market* so you can be sure what you put in comes out clean
    • 4-blade stainless steel chopper disintegrates food particles so they don’t end up sticking to your dishes and glassware
    • Full stainless steel tub built tough enough to handle high heat and resist cracking and staining for years
    • End-of-cycle countdown tracks the time remaining in your wash cycle with a three-digit display on the console
     

  • CLEANING FOR THE LONG HAUL

    Our industry's most powerful motor available* is tested at 2x the life of an average dishwasher.** And our stainless steel chopping blades, nylon racks and stainless steel tub are all backed by a 10-year Limited Parts Warranty.*** So all of your dishes can get a dependable clean, wash after wash. See warranty for complete details.

  • POWERBLAST™ CYCLE

    The PowerBlast™ cycle can handle whatever you throw at it, from your messiest pans to your favorite glassware. It uses high-pressure spray jets and increased temperature to remove tough, stuck-on food and hot steam to finish the job.
  •  

  • Size/Capacity

    Maytag gives you the flexibility to fit even the largest, toughest loads. The tiered upper rack is designed to hold plates and taller items. Plus, premium rack adjusters allow you to move the rack up and down to fit glassware, cookware and more.

  • Save Energy
    & Resources

    ENERGY STAR® qualified dishwashers use less than half the amount of water it takes to wash dishes by hand, and can help save lots of water per year. Add these resource savings to the time you´ll save versus washing by hand, and you´ve got a highly efficient combination.
  •  

  • Measurements

    Before you buy, measure the dimensions of the cutout where your dishwasher will be installed, and any entryways and doors that it will be delivered through. Also check the dishwasher installation guide for power and water source requirements.

    Select models are quieter than your current dishwasher, guaranteed.***
