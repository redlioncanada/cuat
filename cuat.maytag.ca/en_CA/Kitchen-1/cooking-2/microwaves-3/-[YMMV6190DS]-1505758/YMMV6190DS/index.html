EvenAir™ Convection Mode

Electronic Temperature Control

Cook up family favorites in the microwave by setting temperatures like you would in the oven. Electronic temperature controls let you follow a traditional recipe and enter a temperature, so you won't have to worry about guessing cook times and power levels. It's convection cooking with confidence—the way it's meant to be done.
