KitchenAid® Table de cuisson au gaz 48 po, style commercial avec gril KitchenAid®, 6 brûleurs

Cette table de cuisson de 48 po possède un style commercial et offre un rendement comparable à celui des appareils utilisés dans les restaurants. La surface de cuisson à 6 brûleurs comprend deux brûleurs à flamme double Ultra Power™ d'une puissance maximale de 20 000 BTU. Pour plus de souplesse, le gril au gaz Even-Heat™ vous permet de savourer des grillades pendant toute l'année.

Commercial-Style Design with Bold, Heavy-Duty Look and Robust, Die-Cast Black Control Knobs

Provides you with the look and performance that comes from professional, restaurant-quality appliances.

Two 20K BTU Ultra Power™ Dual-Flame Burners

Our most powerful and versatile burners give you two levels of gas flame. At full power, each burner can generate up to 20,000 BTUs for searing and stir-frying.

Three 15K BTU Professional Burners

Give you the power to quickly boil water, perform high-heat cooking techniques, and get restaurant-quality results at both high and low temperatures.

Caractéristiques additionnelles...

5K BTU Simmer/Melt Burner

Allows you to keep your delicate sauces and gravies warm without scorching and at its lowest setting it can be used for melting.

18K BTU Even-Heat™ Gas Grill

Brings the flavors and results of outdoor grilling indoors. Key features include a wave tray and 18K BTU log burner to evenly distribute heat.

Continuous Surface with Heavy-Duty Grates

Make it easy to move cookware from one burner to another without having to lift them. The durable cast-iron grates add to the commercial appeal.

Trousse de conversion PL incluse

Permet de convertir la table de cuisson afin qu'elle puisse être utilisée avec du gaz PL.
