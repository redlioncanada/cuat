KitchenAid® Four simple encastré à convection véritable 24 po kitchenAid®, série Architect® II

Classement

Ce four encastré de 24 po possède une capacité de 3,1 pi cu, idéal pour les cuisines de petite dimension ou en tant que second four pour les grandes cuisines. Le four à convection véritable est doté du système de conversion EasyConvect™ qui élimine les approximations de la conversion du degré de température et de durée de cuissondes recettes conventionnelles.

True Convection Oven

Features a heating element hidden behind a baffle that helps provide more even temperature management. The fan draws air in, past the heating element, and back into the oven.

EasyConvect™ Conversion System

Takes guesswork out of convection cooking by ensuring you can get exceptional results when experimenting with new or familiar recipes.

Closed-Door Thermal Broil with Maxi and Econo Settings

Decreases the time needed to get exceptional results similar to outdoor grilling. Two settings deliver intense, concentrated broiling for large or small quantities.

Caractéristiques additionnelles...

Temperature Probe

Allows accurate measurement of internal temperature of meats, poultry, and casseroles without having to open the oven door.

Electronic Controls with Control Lock Function

Makes the oven easy to use. The intuitive left-to-right display makes it easy to control all oven functions and includes a control lock function that makes it easy to clean and prevents unwanted operation.

Capacité de 3,1 pi cu

Offre une ample capacité pouvant accueillir plusieurs plats en même temps.
