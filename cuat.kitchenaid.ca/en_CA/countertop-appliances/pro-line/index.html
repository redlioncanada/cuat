Pro Line® Series Appliances

The KitchenAid® Pro Line® Series is amongst the highest performing countertop appliances in the world. Engineers and culinary designers collaborated to create precise movements, incredible power, quiet operation and easy-to-navigate controls to ensure professional-style results every time. Die-cast materials with premium finishes add durability and a distinctive look to any countertop.

THE MIXER THAT

INDUSTRY IS AT IT AGAIN

KSM7586P

A timeless classic has just been elegantly elevated. Introducing the KitchenAid® Pro Line® Series 7-Quart Bowl Lift Stand Mixer. Enjoy professional results masterfully blended with distinctive counter style. It's our most powerful, largest capacity, best-performing mixer.

  • 1.3hp motor to run longer with optimum torque and less heat
  • Largest capacity to make 14 dozen cookies, or 8.5 lbs of dough
  • Advanced technology and all-metal gears provide long-life
  • Advanced controls communicate 14,000 more times per second than our 6-quart models, delivering more power to the bowl

   

 

ELEVATED TECHNOLOGY FOR
ELEVATED TOAST

KMT4203, KMT2203

Introducing our most advanced toaster ever. The new Pro Line® Series Automatic Toaster is equipped with a host of smart features for effortless toasting, like our exclusive Keep Warm function which automatically keeps toast warm even if you're not there.

  • Auto-lift and lower
  • Auto Keep Warm
  • "A Little Longer" for one shade darker toast

   

BLEND POWERFULLY,
BLEND ANYWHERE

KHB3581

Hold innovation in the palm of your hand. The KitchenAid® Pro Line® Series Cordless Hand Blender, with its rechargable 12V Lithium-ion battery, is as powerful our corded hand blender and delivers premium performance and consistant power. With the Lithium-ion battery, the hand blender is always ready. Blend, chop, whisk, shred, froth and puree easier than before.

  • Rechargeable Lithium-ion battery for optimal runtime, performance, and durability
  • Industry-first interchangeable bell blade assemblies with no tools required
  • 5 attachements. 5 speeds, 2 blending arms for ultimate versatility

   

 

FINE TUNED ENGINEERING
FOR FINE DICING AND SLICING

KFP1642

Introducing the first food processor with hands-free, commercial-style dicing, calibrated for your countertop. The new 16-cup Pro Line® Series Food Processor debuts an advanced dicing mechanism and the exclusive ExactSlice™ System, to slice from thick to thin with one slice of the lever.

  • Commercial-style dicing
  • 3-in-1 Ultra-Wide Mouth™ Feed Tube for all shapes and sizes
  • ExactSlice™ System with first-ever external adjustable blade control

   

 

BUILT FOR SPEED AND FOR

AND DARJEELING

KEK1522

Introducing the Pro Line® Series Kettle. It quietly boils water in minutes. The variable temperature control allows you to select the exact temperature you desire to help achive optimal results. The dual wall construction helps keep the water temperature hotter for longer, while keeping the outside of the kettle cool to the touch.

  • 1.5 L capacity
  • 1440-Watts to heat water quickly and quietly
  • Temperature control and gauge for precise results

   

 

INDEPENDENT DUAL BOILERS
FOR THE INDEPENDENT BARISTA

KES2102

Brew Espresso and steam milk at the same time with the KitchenAid® Pro Line® Series Espresso Maker featuring Dual Independent Boilers. This unit comes equiped with a variety of premium features to make each and every experience a delight. Such as an adjustable frothing arm, cup warmer and rail and frothing pitcher specially designed to create latte art.

  • Quick heating to reach a boil in less than 6 minutes
  • Dual independent boilers to switch from brewing to frothing instantly
  • Hot water dispenser used to make Americanos, tea, hot chocolate and other drinks
