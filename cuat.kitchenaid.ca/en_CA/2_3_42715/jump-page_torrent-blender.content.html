DOUBLE PEA SOUP

INGREDIENTS

Makes 4-6 servings

1

tablespoon canola or vegetable oil

1

onion, finely chopped

3

cloves garlic, mixed

6

cups water or vegetable broth*

2

cups dried split peas

1

bay leaf

1

teaspoon ground mustard

1

cups frozen green peas

1

teaspoon salt

INSTRUCTIONS

1

*If using broth, reduce the amount of salt to ¼ teaspoon. Taste and add more, if desired.

2

Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion, cook 5 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally. Add garlic, sauté 1 minute.

3

Add water, split peas, bay leaf and mustard, bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer 45 minutes or until split peas are tender, stirring occasionally and adding additional water if necessary.

4

Stir in green peas, salt and pepper; cover and simmer 10 minutes or until green peas are tender. Remove and discard bay leaf.

5

Transfer soup to pitcher of KitchenAid® Torrent™ Blender. Cover and blend on Soups/Sauces setting. Swirl a dollop of sour cream onto each serving, if desired.

CLASSIC MARGARITA

INGREDIENTS

Makes 2 servings

4

ounces Tequila

2

ounces Triple Sec

2

ounces Fresh Lime or Lemon Juice

Lime slices

Course Salt

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Rub rim of two glasses with lime slices; dip in salt.

2

Place ice, tequila, triple sec and lime juice in pitcher of KitchenAid® Torrent™ Blender. Cover and blend on Icy Drinks/Smoothies setting.

3

Pour into prepared glasses. Garnish with lime slices. Serve immediately.

MAGNETIC DRIVE

1.8 PEAK HP*. 18,000 RPMS.

The Magnetic Drive system securely locks the pitcher in place during operation.
Fortyeight permanent magnets connect the pitcher to the base, transferring power from the motor into the pitcher. Unlike other blenders, the pitcher slides in and out like a coffee maker, so there's no need to move it out from underneath kitchen cabinets, twist the pitcher into place, or hold down the lid during blending.
