Additional Features...

8 Wash Cycles

Match the cycle to the load at hand. 8 wash cycles, from Delicates to Heavy Duty, let you select the right cleaning for each load.

Flexible Wash Options

Customize the wash cycle for optimal cleaning.

Automatic Temperature Controls

Promotes dependable cleaning performance by ensuring correct water temperatures.

Precision Balancing System

Uses springs and shock absorbers to reduce vibration and noise in the washer, keeping loads balanced during the cycle.

2.0 cu. ft. Capacity

A smaller washer doesn't have to mean smaller loads. This compact washer provides enough space for full loads of laundry while still fitting in tight spaces.
